About

Why you should make a Quiz ?

The ultimate reason of making Quizzes is growth.

  1. Business growth-entrepreneurs growing by acquiring and retaining customers
  2. Personal or professional growth-teachers or corporate trainers helping people with new knowledge and skills.

Now the Question arises how can this website is used for facilitating this growth?

  1. Marketers can use quizzes as part of a social media marketing strategy to generate brand awareness or to drive engagement.
  2. Organizations also use quizzes to strengthen lessons from onboarding or training.
  3. Project management software platforms, like Wrike, create quizzes for customers after product tutorials. As a result they reduced product training time by 70%.
  4. Teachers and trainers can create a quiz to make lessons more interactive and engaging.

It's been researched and proven that regular quizzes-not to be graded or evaluated boost memory retention, drive engagement, and make learning fun. More on this in a bit.
